Legends of Aragon is a P2E game launched on a web 3.0 platform where players will embody the role of a Templar knight who will prove his skill and worth by raising dragons and mythical beasts. His beasts will engage in hand-to-hand combat to win trophies in the form of nft tokens. Inspired by the collectible card game Magic: the Gathering, BEUROP offers a metaverse where knights and dragons collaborate to be rewarded for breeding, exploring, hunting, and more. The game offers features that allow players to create new beasts and trade them for cryptocurrencies. By trading, players can increase the value of their in-game assets. Legends of Aragon encourages players to explore more in-game content while motivating them to complete their collection of different items, equipment, and pets. Players can then evolve their beasts to advance in the story and increase their assets. The PvP mode allows players to demonstrate their pets' skills in challenging battles. This mode not only encourages players to upgrade their dragons but also to create interactions through the exchange of in-game assets; providing them with a real-life economy.

Dragons

These gigantic and fabulous reptiles have been hated and adored with equal ardor in all cultures. Capable of spitting fire and often flying; dragons have a magical connection to the forces of nature and the universe. Their millenary wisdom has granted them the role of guardian and devourer. Different species vary in shape, size, strength, color, and abilities.

Different species vary in shape, size, strength, color, and abilities. • Dragon (Kayda "Little Dragon") Although small, the scales that cover its body are of unparalleled strength. This reptilian-looking one with bat-like wings spits Greek Fire; a flame that does not die out. Its damage is permanent, and every second exposed to its attack brings you closer to eternal death. Because of its ability to regenerate in seconds, defeating Kayda is almost impossible.

• Wyvern (Tessylth "The Ice Lady") Many have been the stories about the exploits of the great dragons, such as Fafnir, or Smaug, but very few describe the ferocity of the female dragons. Tessylth, of the Wyvern race, expels fire and a blast of cold wind that freezes her enemies. Light as the wind, she can protect herself from enemy fire by flapping her wings and creating a curtain of air.

• Fey Dragon (Glaurung "The Deceiver") Don't let its flying lizard look fool you. Fey Dragons spit green fire and the tips of their claws possess a deadly poison. Immune to magic, they can be truly fearsome if properly trained. Glaurung's only weakness is in close combat.

• Asian (Zu "The Stormbird") Although mythology describes them as benevolent dragons, they are neutral beings that can cause great destruction when enraged. Their remarkable chin allows them to balance and orient themselves in the air and against wind currents. Unlike western dragons, this oriental race spits water, so their attack slows down enemies and stuns them. Its power does not lie in strength but in intelligence; Zu is a fast and intuitive dragon when hunting.

• Hydra (Bryzud "The Lord of Fire") The hydra (Draco hydra) is the only race among dragons, which reproduces by separating heads from its body. They fight using flames that spit from multiple directions, and their bites can be deadly. Renowned for their cannibalism, Bryzud is feared, even by other dragon races.

Levels

Pets can reach three levels, with each level increasing the reward potential. Bronze pet (mystery box) Silver pet (combine 5 bronze pets) Gold pet (3 best players of the PVP tournament)

Mystery Box

Mystery boxes hide not only dragon eggs, but magical secrets that will elevate you from a simple squire to a knight. 1) Bronze box: using BEPR you can buy a mystery box and get a bronze dragon. 2) Silver box: with the game´s token LOAG, buy a bronze or silver dragon. 3) Gold box: if you have a lucky star you can get a silver or gold dragon. Each box gives you a chance to get a pet piece. By collecting a total of six pieces, you can combine a beast, to make her fierce and strong.

Battle modes

To improve your beasts' skills you have to win battles in tournaments. Weekly Player VS Player tournament (top 10 players get an item to increase the pet's skill). Monthly PVP tournament (top 3 players can get a dollar prize and a limited gold pet).

Earn rewards

By sending your pet to the jungle to hunt (stake BEPR) you can receive better returns on investment; staking APY depends on the pet's NFT skill.

Marketplace: buy, sell and trade

The web-based marketplace will be a crucial part of the Legends of Aragon ecosystem, as it will encourage players to work hard to acquire and upgrade their beasts, as well as obtain rare cards. They can also buy dragon eggs to breed and fight them. Dragons with more abilities will have a higher trade value, as well as rare cards that can be found in the Mystery Boxes.
