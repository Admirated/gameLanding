About game

Legends of Aragon

Legends of Aragon is a P2E game launched on a web 3.0 platform
where players will embody the role of a Templar knight who will
prove his skill and worth by raising dragons and mythical beasts.
His beasts will engage in hand-to-hand combat to win trophies
in the form of nft tokens.
Inspired by the collectible card game Magic: the Gathering,
BEUROP offers a metaverse where knights and dragons collaborate
to be rewarded for breeding, exploring, hunting, and more.

The PvP mode allows players to demonstrate their pets'
skills in challenging battles. This mode not only
encourages players to upgrade their dragons but also to
create interactions through the exchange of in-game
assets; providing them with a real-life economy.
